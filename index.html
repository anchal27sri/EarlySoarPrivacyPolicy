Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how EarlySoar ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application "EarlySoar: The Learning App" (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information, especially for children under the age of 13.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected from Students/Children

When a student or child uses our App, we may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Age
  • Class/Grade level
  • School name
  • Address
  • Parents' or guardians' names
  • Parents' or guardians' phone numbers
  • Parents' or guardians' email addresses

1.2 Information Collected from Teachers/Adults

When a teacher or adult user uses our App, we may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • School/class assignment information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our educational services
  • To monitor student progress and learning outcomes
  • To communicate with parents, guardians, and teachers
  • To ensure the safety and security of our users
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage

All data collected through the App is stored securely in an internal SQL database. This database is used exclusively for monitoring purposes, tracking student progress, and facilitating communication between students, parents, and teachers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT share, sell, or rent your personal information to any third parties or advertisers. Your data remains confidential and is used solely for the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy. We do not engage in any form of data monetization or advertising partnerships that would involve sharing your personal information.

The only circumstances under which we may disclose information are:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of EarlySoar, our users, or others
  • With your explicit consent

5. Children's Privacy Protection (COPPA Compliance)

EarlySoar is committed to protecting the privacy of children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), which requires us to obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting, using, or disclosing personal information from children under the age of 13.

5.1 Verifiable Parental Consent (VPC)

Before we collect any personal information from a child under 13, we require verifiable parental consent. This means that a parent or guardian must:

  • Review this Privacy Policy
  • Provide explicit consent for their child's use of the App
  • Verify their identity through a secure process

We will not collect, use, or disclose personal information from children under 13 without obtaining verifiable parental consent first.

5.2 Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the following rights regarding their child's information:

  • Right to Review: Parents can request to review the personal information we have collected about their child
  • Right to Delete: Parents can request that we delete their child's personal information from our systems
  • Right to Revoke Consent: Parents can revoke their consent at any time, after which we will stop collecting, using, or disclosing their child's information
  • Right to Refuse Further Collection: Parents can refuse to allow further collection or use of their child's information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided at the end of this Privacy Policy.

6. App Permissions

6.1 Internet Permission

The App requires Internet permission to:

  • Connect to our secure SQL database to store and retrieve user data
  • Deliver educational content, learning materials, and updates
  • Enable real-time synchronization of progress and activities
  • Facilitate communication features within the App

All data transmitted over the Internet is encrypted to ensure security and privacy.

6.2 Audio/Microphone Permission

The App requires Audio/Microphone permission solely for:

  • Interactive learning activities
  • Pronunciation exercises and language learning
  • Voice-activated educational activities
  • Speech recognition features for educational purposes

Important: Audio recordings are processed in real-time for educational purposes only. Audio data is NOT permanently stored for marketing purposes, and it is NOT shared with third parties or advertisers. Audio data may be temporarily stored only as necessary to provide the educational features of the App, and is deleted after processing.

7. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. These measures include:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure database storage
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
